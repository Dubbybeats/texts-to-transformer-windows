# Community code of conduct

This project welcomes people learning in public, including people who have never trained a model or
contributed to open source before.

Be patient, kind, and specific. Assume questions are sincere. Explain unfamiliar terms when they are
necessary. Do not mock someone for their hardware, operating system, spelling, experience level, or
accessibility needs.

Respect privacy. Never pressure anyone to share conversations, databases, screenshots, logs with
personal paths, trained models, or other private artifacts. Help people create synthetic
reproductions instead.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, and deliberate privacy
violations are not acceptable. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict
participation when needed to protect the community.

If you experience or witness harmful behavior, report it privately to the repository owner rather
than escalating publicly. Good-faith reports will be handled with care and confidentiality.
